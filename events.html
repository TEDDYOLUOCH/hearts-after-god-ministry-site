Event FAQ

How do I register for an event?

Click the "Details & Register" button on any event to sign up online.

Can I host my own event?

Absolutely! Use the "Host an Event" button above to submit your event idea.

Are events free?

Most events are free, but some may require registration or a small fee. Details are listed on each event.

How can I volunteer?

Click the "Volunteer" button above to sign up and get involved.

Where are events held?

All our events are held online via Zoom and YouTube Live. Links are provided when you register for each event.

How do I get event updates?

Subscribe for updates using the button above or follow us on social media.
