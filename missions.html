Our Mission

Hearts After God Ministry is committed to spreading the Gospel and serving communities worldwide. Our mission teams travel locally and internationally, sharing Christ's love through evangelism, humanitarian aid, discipleship, and practical service.

  • Organizing evangelistic crusades and outreach events
  • Supporting local churches and new believers
  • Providing food, clothing, and medical aid to those in need
  • Training and equipping mission volunteers
  • Partnering with ministries and organizations globally

How to Join a Mission

  1. Pray about your calling and availability
  2. Contact our Missions Team for upcoming opportunities
  3. Attend a missions orientation and training session
  4. Participate in fundraising and team-building activities
  5. Go and serve with love, humility, and boldness!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join a mission trip?

Anyone with a heart for service and a willingness to learn can join! We welcome all ages and backgrounds.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior missions experience is required. We provide training and support for all volunteers.

How are mission trips funded?

Mission trips are funded through donations, fundraising, and personal contributions. We guide you through the process.

Where do you serve?

We serve locally and internationally, wherever God leads and doors open for ministry.
