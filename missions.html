Our Mission & Vision

Hearts After God Ministry Missions exists to share the love and message of Jesus Christ with every nation, serve the vulnerable, and empower believers to fulfill the Great Commission through compassion, evangelism, and discipleship.

Our Mission

To go, send, and support missionaries and outreach teams, making disciples and transforming lives for Christ in every community and nation.

Our Vision

To see a global family of believers empowered, equipped, and united in reaching the world for Jesus through love, service, and revival.

Our Journey

  1. 19th September 2024

    The group began as a few friends met to pray together, driven by a hunger and thirst for God's presence. From these prayers, the group was birthed to give an opportunity for other thirsty souls around the world to come together and seek the presence of God.

  2. 22nd May 2025

    Discipleship class began to help equip new believers in their walk with Christ.
