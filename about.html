Our Story

❤️ About Hearts After God Ministry

Hearts After God Ministry was founded on September 19th, 2024, in Kakamega, Kenya, by a group of Christian friends united by a passion for deeper fellowship, prayer, and discipleship rooted in the Word of God.

What began as a small WhatsApp group quickly blossomed into a vibrant online ministry, connecting believers through regular prayer sessions, monthly gatherings, and structured discipleship classes that nurture spiritual growth and community.

On May 21st, 2025, the vision expanded under the leadership of Cyrilla Chikamai, embracing digital evangelism to reach hearts worldwide through blogs, articles, and online platforms—empowering believers to live out their faith wherever they are.

Our Mission

Our online ministry cultivates an accessible global platform, designed to enable individuals worldwide to forge partnerships, experience profound fellowship, and deepen their intimacy with the Holy Spirit through consistent engagement with scriptural teachings and dedicated prayer.

Acts 2:17

"And it shall come to pass in the last days says the God, that I'll pour out my spirit on all flesh, your sons and daughters shall prophesy, your young men shall see visions, your old men shall dream dreams."

Our Vision

Our fundamental objective is to facilitate the realization of God's divine plans for individuals' lives, empowering them to function fully in His supernatural strength, compassion, and spiritual governance. Through this empowerment, we aim to actively convey and implement God's desires for people globally, thereby drawing an increasing number of human hearts into a relationship with Him.

Matthew 28:19-20

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you, and lo, I am with you always to the close of the age."
