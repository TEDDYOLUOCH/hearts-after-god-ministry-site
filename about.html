About Hearts After God Ministry

Empowering Believers. Birthing Ministries. Transforming Nations.

A global family passionate about soul-winning, discipleship, and revival. Our mission: "To ignite revival, equip disciples, and birth ministries for the transformation of nations."

Our Journey

A timeline of God's faithfulness

  1. 19th September 2024 Prayer Group Birthed A group of friends met to pray together because of the hunger and thirst for the presence of God in their hearts, and from the prayers the group was birthed to give an opportunity for other thirsty souls around the world to come together and seek for the presence of God.
  2. 22nd May 2025 Discipleship Class Launched Discipleship class began to help equip new believers in their walk with Christ.

Our Vision, Values & Foundation

The principles that guide our ministry

Prayer & Worship We cultivate a lifestyle of passionate prayer and worship, seeking God's presence above all.
Discipleship & Growth We equip believers to grow in faith, character, and leadership for kingdom impact.
Global Missions We send and support missionaries, birthing ministries and transforming nations for Christ.
Holy Spirit Partnership We depend on the Holy Spirit for guidance, power, and supernatural results in all we do.
Community & Service We serve our city and world with practical love, compassion, and generosity.

Scriptural Foundation

  • Acts 1:8 – "You will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses... to the ends of the earth."
  • Matthew 28:19-20 – "Go and make disciples of all nations..."
  • Ephesians 4:11-13 – "To equip his people for works of service, so that the body of Christ may be built up..."
