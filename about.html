Our Story

From humble beginnings to a global ministry reaching souls across nations

The Vision & Calling

"But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth."
â€” Acts 1:8

Hearts After God Ministry was birthed from a divine vision to facilitate soul-winning, discipleship, and revival globally. Our founder, Minister Cyrilla Chikamai, received a clear calling to create a platform where believers could learn, unlearn, and relearn in their walk with the Holy Spirit.

We are passionate about empowering believers, unlocking destinies, and birthing new ministries as God leads. Our mission extends beyond traditional church boundaries to reach souls worldwide through innovative discipleship programs and Spirit-filled ministry.

Our Journey

2015

Ministry Foundation

Hearts After God Ministry was officially established with a vision for global revival and discipleship. The ministry began with small gatherings focused on Spirit-filled worship and teaching.

2018

Discipleship Program Launch

Launched our comprehensive 14-Day Discipleship Training Program, designed to root new believers and equip them for ministry. This marked a significant milestone in our mission to disciple believers.

2020

Global Missions Expansion

Expanded our "Glow for Christ" missions ministry, reaching souls across multiple nations. The ministry began to see significant growth in international outreach and evangelism.

2023

Digital Ministry Platform

Launched comprehensive digital ministry platforms, including online discipleship programs, virtual worship services, and global outreach initiatives, reaching thousands worldwide.

2025

Revival & Growth

Celebrating 10+ years of ministry with over 500 disciples trained, 1000+ souls reached, and 6 active ministries serving communities worldwide. Continuing our mission for global revival.

Our Core Values

The foundational principles that guide everything we do in ministry

Christ-Centered Living

Everything we do is centered on Jesus Christ. We prioritize His teachings, follow His example, and seek to glorify Him in all aspects of our ministry and personal lives.

Spirit-Led Revival

We are committed to Spirit-filled ministry that brings revival and transformation. Our worship, teaching, and outreach are guided by the Holy Spirit's leading.

Discipleship & Mentorship

We believe in the power of discipleship and mentorship to transform lives. Our structured programs help believers grow in faith and prepare for ministry.

Global Missions

Our heart is for global outreach and missions. We actively engage in evangelism, support international ministries, and participate in God's work worldwide.

Community & Fellowship

We foster authentic community and fellowship where believers can grow together, support one another, and experience the love of Christ in practical ways.

Ministry Multiplication

We believe in birthing and supporting new ministries. Our goal is to equip and empower believers to start their own ministries and expand God's kingdom.
