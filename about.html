Who We Are

Hearts After God Ministry is a Christ-centered, Bible-believing community dedicated to raising a generation after God's heart. Founded in prayer and fellowship, we have grown into a vibrant family of believers passionate about worship, discipleship, and outreach. Our mission is to glorify God, build up the body of Christ, and share His love with the world.

Our Vision

To raise a generation after God's heart, impacting families, communities, and nations for Christ.

Our Purpose

To glorify God, build up the body of Christ, and share His love with the world.

Our Mission

To disciple, equip, and send believers to fulfill the Great Commission through worship, teaching, and acts of love.

What We Believe

We believe that salvation is God's greatest treasure, received by grace through faith in Jesus Christ. The result is a changed life, peace with God, and the promise of eternal life.

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God and our authority for faith and life.
  • Jesus Christ is the Son of God, Savior, and Lord.
  • Salvation is by grace through faith, leading to repentance and new life.
  • The Holy Spirit empowers believers for holy living and service.
  • We are called to love God, love people, and make disciples of all nations.
Read More
  • We believe in the unity of the body of Christ across all nations and backgrounds.
  • We practice baptism and communion as taught in Scripture.
  • We value prayer, worship, and the gifts of the Holy Spirit.
  • We are committed to serving our communities and sharing the Gospel.

Our Founding Story & Divine Calling

Hearts After God Ministry was birthed from a season of deep prayer and a clear divine mandate to raise a generation after God's heart. The Lord impressed upon our founders the urgency of soul-winning, discipleship, and equipping believers to walk in their God-given purpose. Through many confirmations and miraculous doors, the ministry began as a small fellowship and has grown into a global movement, impacting lives across nations.

Leadership Team: Our Spiritual Journey

Our leaders have each experienced a unique journey of faith, marked by encounters with God, seasons of growth, and a passion to serve. Their testimonies inspire and guide the ministry family.

Vision: Birthing New Ministries

Hearts After God is not just a ministry, but a platform for birthing new ministries and callings. We believe God is raising up leaders, worshipers, teachers, and evangelists through this family—each equipped to launch new works and reach new communities for Christ. Our vision is to see many ministries, churches, and outreach teams birthed and sent out from this movement.

God's Manifestation Through This Platform

Through Hearts After God, we have witnessed God's hand in many dimensions—healing, deliverance, prophetic words, restoration of families, and supernatural provision. The ministry is a testimony to God's faithfulness and the power of the Holy Spirit at work in ordinary people.
