About Hearts After God Ministry

A Christ-centered revival ministry called to reach the world, disciple believers, and birth new ministries for God's glory.

Our Vision & Calling

"But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth."
— Acts 1:8

Hearts After God Ministry exists to facilitate soul-winning, discipleship, and revival globally. We are passionate about empowering believers, unlocking destinies, and birthing new ministries as God leads.

Our mission is to provide a platform for learning, unlearning, and relearning, supporting believers in their walk with the Holy Spirit and equipping them for global impact.
