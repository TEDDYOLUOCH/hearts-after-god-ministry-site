About Hearts After God Ministry

Empowering Believers. Birthing Ministries. Transforming Nations.

A global family passionate about soul-winning, discipleship, and revival. Our mission: "To ignite revival, equip disciples, and birth ministries for the transformation of nations."

Our Journey

  1. 2015 Prayer Group Founded A handful of believers gather to pray for revival and global impact.
  2. 2017 First Discipleship Class The 14-Day Discipleship Classes launch, equipping new believers for ministry.
  3. 2019 Glow for Christ Missions First international mission teams sent out, birthing new ministries abroad.
  4. 2021 Community Outreach Milestone Over 5,000 meals served and 1,000+ families reached through local outreach.
  5. 2023 Global Movement Hearts After God becomes a global platform for revival, discipleship, and ministry birthing.
  6. 2024 Recognized for Global Impact Awarded for outstanding service and ministry innovation in multiple nations.

Our Vision, Values & Foundation

Prayer & Worship We cultivate a lifestyle of passionate prayer and worship, seeking God's presence above all.
Discipleship & Growth We equip believers to grow in faith, character, and leadership for kingdom impact.
Global Missions We send and support missionaries, birthing ministries and transforming nations for Christ.
Holy Spirit Partnership We depend on the Holy Spirit for guidance, power, and supernatural results in all we do.
Community & Service We serve our city and world with practical love, compassion, and generosity.

Scriptural Foundation

  • Acts 1:8 – "You will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses... to the ends of the earth."
  • Matthew 28:19-20 – "Go and make disciples of all nations..."
  • Ephesians 4:11-13 – "To equip his people for works of service, so that the body of Christ may be built up..."

What Makes Us Unique?

A Family, Not Just a Fellowship

We are a global family united by love, purpose, and the Holy Spirit. Every member is valued, equipped, and empowered to fulfill their God-given destiny.

Ministry Birthing Platform

We don't just grow churches—we birth ministries, support new leaders, and launch kingdom initiatives worldwide.

Supernatural Culture

We pursue revival, healing, prophecy, and miracles as a normal part of Christian life, always rooted in the Word and love.
