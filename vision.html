Our Mission

Mission Statement
"Hearts After God Ministry offers an online platform with the aim of reaching souls worldwide and facilitating fellowship with the Holy Spirit through prayers and the Word to usher revival, empower and root new believers through discipleship, unlock God-envisioned destinies, and provide a platform to share life lessons for learning, unlearning, and relearning."

Mission Objectives & Soul-Winning Goals

Our comprehensive approach to fulfilling the Great Commission through digital evangelism, discipleship, and global ministry support.

1

Global Digital Evangelism

Reach souls globally through digital evangelism and outreach, leveraging technology to spread the Gospel worldwide.

2

Holy Spirit Fellowship

Facilitate fellowship with the Holy Spirit through prayer and the Word, creating spaces for divine encounter.

3

Revival & Awakening

Usher revival and spiritual awakening in individuals and communities, igniting passion for Christ.

4

Intentional Discipleship

Empower and root new believers through intentional discipleship programs and spiritual mentoring.

5

Ministry Multiplication

Birth new ministries and support their growth worldwide, creating a network of Kingdom-focused organizations.

6

Destiny Unlocking

Unlock God-envisioned destinies and empower believers to serve in their unique callings.

7

Life Lessons Platform

Provide a platform for sharing life lessons and testimonies for learning, unlearning, and relearning.

8

Global Family

Foster a global family passionate about revival, discipleship, and soul-winning across all nations.
