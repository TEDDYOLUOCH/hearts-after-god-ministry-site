Our Vision, Purpose & Mission

“So we, being many, are one body in Christ, and every one members one of another.”
Romans 12:5

Vision & Mission Core Values Ministry Focus Global Reach Discipleship Soul Winning

Vision

"To give this platform to God as an opportunity to use us to reach souls globally (Acts 1:8). More ministries will be birthed from Hearts After God, and God will manifest Himself in different dimensions to the world through it."
Acts 1:8 Foundation

"But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit has come upon you, and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the end of the earth."

Mission

"Hearts After God Ministry offers an online platform with the aim of reaching souls worldwide and facilitating fellowship with the Holy Spirit through prayers and the Word to usher revival, empower and root new believers through discipleship, unlock God-envisioned destinies, and provide a platform to share life lessons for learning, unlearning, and relearning."
  • Global soul-winning and outreach
  • Facilitating Holy Spirit fellowship
  • Empowering and rooting new believers through discipleship
  • Unlocking destinies and birthing new ministries
  • Sharing life lessons for growth and transformation

Core Values & Ministry Approach

  • Holy Spirit Fellowship: We prioritize a deep relationship with the Holy Spirit in all we do.
  • Discipleship & Revival: Empowering and rooting new believers, ushering revival through prayer and the Word.
  • Birthing New Ministries: Equipping and sending out leaders, worshipers, and evangelists to launch new works.
  • Destiny Unlocking: Helping believers discover and walk in their God-given purpose.
  • Transformation & Growth: Providing a platform for learning, unlearning, and relearning in Christ.

What We Believe

We believe that salvation is God’s greatest treasure, received by grace through faith in Jesus Christ. The result is a changed life, peace with God, and the promise of eternal life.

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God and our authority for faith and life.
  • Jesus Christ is the Son of God, Savior, and Lord.
  • Salvation is by grace through faith, leading to repentance and new life.
  • The Holy Spirit empowers believers for holy living and service.
  • We are called to love God, love people, and make disciples of all nations.

Guiding Principles

We are committed to the apostles’ doctrine, fellowship, breaking of bread, and prayers (Acts 2:42).
We seek to live holy and Spirit-led lives, reflecting Christ in all we do.
We value unity in the body of Christ and serve one another in love.
We are called to share the Gospel and make disciples of all nations.

How We Worship & Live

Our worship is Christ-centered, Spirit-led, and rooted in the Word. We gather regularly for prayer, teaching, fellowship, and the breaking of bread. Our lifestyle is marked by love, humility, service, and a desire to honor God in every area of life.

  • Regular worship, prayer, and Bible study
  • Active participation in fellowship and community
  • Living out our faith through service and outreach
  • Pursuing holiness and spiritual growth
