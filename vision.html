Vision & Mission

"You will be my witnesses... to the ends of the earth." (Acts 1:8)

Our calling is global. Our foundation is the Word. Our passion is revival.

Our Vision

"To give this platform to God as an opportunity to use us to reach souls globally (Acts 1:8). More ministries will be birthed from Hearts After God, and God will manifest Himself in different dimensions to the world through it."
Scriptural Foundation

“But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth.” — Acts 1:8

Our Mission

"Hearts After God Ministry offers an online platform with the aim of reaching souls worldwide and facilitating fellowship with the Holy Spirit through prayers and the Word to usher revival, empower and root new believers through discipleship, unlock God-envisioned destinies, and provide a platform to share life lessons for learning, unlearning, and relearning."

Mission Objectives & Soul-Winning Goals

  • 1 Reach souls globally through digital evangelism and outreach.
  • 2 Facilitate fellowship with the Holy Spirit through prayer and the Word.
  • 3 Usher revival and spiritual awakening in individuals and communities.
  • 4 Empower and root new believers through intentional discipleship.
  • 5 Birth new ministries and support their growth worldwide.
  • 6 Unlock God-envisioned destinies and empower believers to serve.
  • 7 Provide a platform for sharing life lessons and testimonies.
  • 8 Foster a global family passionate about revival, discipleship, and soul-winning.

Our Core Values

Fellowship with the Holy Spirit

We prioritize a deep, daily relationship with the Holy Spirit in all we do.

Discipleship & Growth

We are committed to equipping and rooting believers for lifelong growth and service.

Global Missions

We exist to reach the world for Christ, birthing new ministries and supporting global outreach.

Revival & Prayer

We believe in the power of prayer and seek continual revival in the church and world.

Community & Service

We value authentic community, love, and service, reflecting Christ's heart to the world.

Empowerment & Destiny

We are passionate about unlocking destinies and empowering believers to fulfill God's call.

Our Ministry Approach

Discipleship & Revival

  • Intentional discipleship programs for new and growing believers
  • Revival-focused gatherings and prayer initiatives
  • Empowering believers to walk in the Spirit and fulfill their calling

Birthing New Ministries

  • Supporting the launch and growth of new ministries worldwide
  • Mentoring and equipping ministry leaders
  • Providing resources and a platform for ministry collaboration

Destiny Unlocking & Empowerment

We believe every believer has a God-envisioned destiny. Through discipleship, prayer, and the power of the Holy Spirit, we help unlock and empower these destinies for the glory of God and the transformation of the world.

Empower. Equip. Release.
