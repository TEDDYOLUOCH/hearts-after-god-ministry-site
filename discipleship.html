Grow deeper in your faith, discover your calling, and be equipped to make disciples. Join our transformative 14-day journey!

About the Program

Our 14-day discipleship classes are designed to help you grow in Christ, build spiritual habits, and connect with a supportive community. Each day features teaching, discussion, and practical application.

  • Daily teaching and devotionals
  • Small group discussions
  • Personal mentorship
  • Resource downloads

Curriculum Overview

Day 1: Foundations of Faith

Understanding salvation, grace, and your new identity in Christ.

  • Key Scripture: Ephesians 2:8-10
  • Download: Day 1 Notes (PDF)

Day 2: Prayer & Devotion

Building a daily habit of prayer, worship, and Bible study.

  • Key Scripture: Matthew 6:6
  • Download: Day 2 Devotional (PDF)

Day 3: Community & Fellowship

The importance of spiritual family and accountability.

  • Key Scripture: Hebrews 10:24-25
  • Download: Day 3 Guide (PDF)

Days 4-14: Growing & Going

Practical steps for spiritual growth, sharing your faith, and serving others.
